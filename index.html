It's Streamly simple!

Instructions

Adding videos

First, type in the video you would like to view. A window of YouTube with your search will open. (add a semicolon ";" to stop from searching)

Right-click the video you wish to view and copy its URL. Paste it into the Streamly prompt.

Type the length of the video you have chosen. (you can use spaces instead of colon ":") That's it. No API, no nonsense.

Pausing videos

Click the YouTube video to pause it, then click the pause button on top of the video.

In the same order, click the YouTube video, then click the play button on top to unpause.

Going forward or backward in the playlist

Just click the forward or backward buttons above the video. It will then go to the next or previous video.

(You can also do this by using the left and right arrow keys)

Check out our GitHub repo for more info.
