It's Streamly simple!

Instructions

Adding videos

First, type in the video you would like to view. A window of YouTube with your search will open.

Right-click the video you wish to view and copy its URL. Paste it into the Streamly prompt.

Type the length of the video you have chosen. (you can use spaces instead of ":") That's it. No API, no nonsense.

Pausing videos

Click the YouTube video to pause it, then click the pause button on top of the video.

If you want to reverse the small time change that this causes, click the play button on top first, then click the video to play.

Check out our GitHub repo for more info.
